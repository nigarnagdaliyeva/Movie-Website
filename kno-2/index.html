МОСКВА — КАССИОПЕЯ

Moscow-Cassiopeia

DIRECTOR
Richard Viktorov

Moscow-Cassiopeia" is a 1974 Soviet science fiction film directed by Richard Viktorov. The story follows a group of talented teenagers selected for a space mission to the distant Cassiopeia constellation. As they travel through space, they encounter unexpected challenges and adventures, making the film a beloved classic of Soviet cinema. The cast includes Misha Yershov as Vitya Sereda, Sasha Grigoriev as Pasha Kozelkov, and Sergey Obrazov as Fedya Lobanov.
